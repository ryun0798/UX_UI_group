Your New Favorite Hobby

Are you looking to find a way to exercise both physically and mentally while enjoying your surroundings? Our app “Geocache” is the exact thing you need to maximize your free time and curiosity! It combines exploring your day to day or foreign location and our cutting edge technology to discover treasure! We have compiled digital maps and our user’s “hidden treasures” for you to uncover solo or with friends! When you’ve had your fill of discovery, you could even hide an eco-friendly item for the community, too!

Virtual Discoveries

Our latest update now features “Augmented Reality” that empowers you to challenge yourself not only outdoors, but indoors too! You can now enjoy a virtual treasure hunt with our in-app AR feature. New, vibrant icons on the map will identify these virtual treasures that you can open simply using your phone’s camera. Not only can you collect these virtual items, but our update also includes “Achievements” that you can unlock while exploring with the Geocache app. These achievements are shared with a network of your friends and fellow explorers that you can add in the app, too. Now, get out there and discover a new adventure!
